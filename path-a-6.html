Nine Lives

Path A — The Office

You ring a small bell that lies on your desk. A fleet of drones forcefully drags the mob out of the office. They're all fired. Meow.

However, something shifts. The universe, having witnessed your ninth life unfolding, has had enough. Even the stars have reached a breaking point.

Stars align into a picket line. The moon calls in sick. The sun files a complaint.

THE UNIVERSE IS ON STRIKE.

You wake up in an alley. It smells like rain. You have one life left.

It seems you must try again.
